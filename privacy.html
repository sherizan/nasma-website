Privacy Policy

At Nasma, your privacy is our highest priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our application and services. By using Nasma, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to improve our services and enhance your experience. Here’s what we collect:

1.1 Information You Provide:

  • Account Details: Name, email address, phone number, and profile photo when you sign up.
  • Messages & Calls: Content and metadata from your chats are stored temporarily for delivery purposes.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, including features used and session duration.
  • Location Data: Approximate location to personalize content or services (optional, with your permission).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

1.1 Information You Provide:

  • Provide and improve our services.
  • Personalize user experiences (e.g., responsive ambient UI).
  • Conduct analytics to understand user behavior and enhance app performance.
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share your information in the following scenarios:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help us with app operations (e.g., hosting, analytics).
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect Nasma’s rights and users.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely on encrypted servers.
  • Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

5. Your Privacy Choices

You have control over your information:

  • Access and Update: Edit your profile or account details at any time.
  • Delete Account: You can request to delete your account and associated data.
  • Opt-Out: Manage permissions for location, notifications, and other data collection via your device settings.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services or comply with legal obligations. For example:

  • Messages are stored temporarily for delivery and then deleted.
  • Account data is retained until you delete your account.

7. Third-Party Services

Nasma may integrate third-party services (e.g., payment processors, analytics tools). These services follow their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

8. Children's Privacy

Nasma is not intended for users under 4 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
