We collect information to improve our services and enhance your experience. Here’s what we collect:

1.2 Automatically Collected Information:
	•	Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version, and unique device identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information to:

4. Data Storage and Security

	•	Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

5. Your Privacy Choices
You have control over your information:

6. Data Retention
We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services or comply with legal obligations. For example:
